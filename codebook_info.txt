"Subject.Number" is integer subject ID ranging from 1 to 30. 
"Activity" is a factor has 6 possible activities. Walking, walking_upstairs, walking_downstairs, sitting, standing,laying.
"Dataset" is a string denoting whether data is from “train”ing set or “test”ing set.

Dataset only includes mean and standard deviation values. Upper group being mean and Lower group being standard deviation. The same amount of variables for mean and std exist.
